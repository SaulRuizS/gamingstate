Cyberpunk 2077 has three different prologues

Author

John Wick

In Cyberpunk 2077 players will be able to choose between three different origin stories, each one presenting a different start location for its character.

Talking with VG247, the designer Pawel Sasko explained a system that Projekt RED calls "Lifepaths", in practice sounds quite similar to the Dragon Age: Origins character origin stories. One of these Lifepaths starts in the Badlands, the arid lands that rounds Night City. CD Project RED has not offered a description of the other areas, but looks wise to suppose that the Corpo's characters could start in a more corporative district of Night City, while the Street Kids could come from Outcasted areas.

News, launch date and Cyberpunk 2077

In other news, we recently discovered that CD Projekt RED plans to develop three Cberpunk games, and that Cyberpunk 2077 will have romantic stories similar to The Witcher 3. If romance is not your style, do not worry, you can be as evil as you want thanks to Cyberpunk 2077 will not have a moral system. Cyberpunk 2077 will be available on april 16th of 2020 for PC, Playstation 4 and Xbox One.
